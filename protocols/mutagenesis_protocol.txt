PCR Mutagenesis Setup

1. Thaw Q5 Hot Start Master Mix, forward primer, reverse primer, plasmid template, and nuclease-free water.
2. Label 2 PCR tubes (Negative Control, Mutagenesis)
3. Prepare the PCR master mix for 2 reactions (12 µL Q5 Hot Start Master Mix, 2 µL Forward Primer (10 µM), 2 µL Reverse Primer (10 µM), 2 µL nuclease-free water)
4. Mix the master mix gently until homogeneous.
5. Add 6 µL master mix to each PCR tube.
6. Add to Mutagenesis 2 µL plasmid template
7. Add to Negative Control 2 µL nuclease-free water
8. Gently pipette-mix each reaction.
9. Briefly spin tubes to collect liquid at the bottom.
10. Place tubes into the thermocycler.
